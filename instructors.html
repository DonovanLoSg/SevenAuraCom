Instructor

Founder of 7 Aura, Dennis Lee has been practicing Hatha Yoga since 1998 under the tutelage of Mazie Staya Kamal. He has since been instructing at various education institutions and stat-boards such as East Shore Hospital, Health Science Authority, Oasis Holistic Yoga Centre, Compassville Primary School and Safra EnergyOne (Tampines & Mount Faber).

Dennis was awarded the international Certificate in Yoga Instructor, International Diploma in Yoga Education by the Institute for Vedic Studies Yoga Education and Research, India (IVYER).

He was guided under the personal instructions under Master Guru Jayant Rangneth Kulkarni. Trained by renowned Australian yoga instructor, Peter Thomson, Dennis is now a certified Iyengar teacher.
