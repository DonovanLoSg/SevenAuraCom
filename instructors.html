The People of 7 Aura

Click on the instructors to learn more about them.

Founder of 7 Aura, Dennis Lee has been practicing Hatha Yoga since 1998 under the tutelage of Mazie Staya Kamal. He has since been instructing at various education institutions and stat-boards such as East Shore Hospital, Health Science Authority, Oasis Holistic Yoga Centre, Compassville Primary School and Safra EnergyOne (Tampines & Mount Faber).

Dennis was awarded the international Certificate in Yoga Instructor, International Diploma in Yoga Education by the Institute for Vedic Studies Yoga Education and Research, India (IVYER).

He was guided under the personal instructions under Master Guru Jayant Rangneth Kulkarni. Trained by renowned Australian yoga instructor, Peter Thomson, Dennis is now a certified Iyengar teacher.

Audrey has been a yoga practitioner since 1991. In 2005, she decided to give Iyengar Yoga a try and has never looked back since.

Under the training and guidiance of Peter Thomson, senior Iyengar Yoga teacher based in Australasia, she became a certified Iyengar Yoga teacher.

Audrey was also a qualified vocational teacher and has been teaching yoga since 2009. Having benefited from various aspects in practicing yoga, her greatest wish is to share the practical knowledge, its benefits and the joy with fellow students and friends in this great art.

Being a believer and a student in lifelong learning, she continues to practice with Peter Thomson to further develop her skills.

Vasanthi obtained her Yoga Instructor Certificate Course from the well acclaimed yoga research institute, Svyasa Yoga Research Institute in Bangalore (India) in 1995. Upon completing her YICC in Bangalore, she continued staying in Svyasa for 5 months to further enhance her knowledge and practice in Yoga. While in Svyasa, she was actively involved in the Personality Development Camp (PDC) for children organising yoga classes and workshops for children.

Upon returning to Singapore, Vasanthi continued working and taught part-time Yoga in several organisations including Overseas Family School and corporations such as Ernst & Young, ESSO Singapore (now known as Exxon Mobile), the National Computer Board, Economic Development Board, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Defence, the Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore,

Singapore Island Country Club and Fort Canning Country Club. When she was posted overseas in Australia and Indonesia, she continued teaching Yoga to her colleagues and friends. In 2004 she left the corporate world and has since been teaching yoga full time to corporate and individuals.

Vasanthi also conducts the Yoga & Ayurveda Teacher’s Training with certificate from the renowned Ayurveda and Yoga Institute – the Institute of Ayurveda and Integrative Medicine, Bangalore (India).

The unintentional practitioner, Chow Tuck Keong has been a yoga enthusiast since 2003. He embraced a professional teacher training course in India in 2005.

Since 2006, he has been training under RYT registered yoga teacher Peter Thomson, as part of a 2-year Iyengar teacher training course of self-growth and continuous exploration. He is keen to share his love of yoga and all that is spiritual, physical and empowering.

Yeoh Su Unn was first attracted to yoga by the physical comforts that it gave her. As she immerse in the practice, the asana work on the mat gradually moved beyond the physical realm and evolved into somethin that nourished and nurtured her entire being. Her practice focuses on developing awareness of existing conditions and from there, adjusting to fulfill the potential.

Su Unn has been studying with Peter Thomson, senior Iyengar teacher, since 2008 and continues to develop her practice under his guidance.

Happy and joyful Chris practices open heart meditation and reiki since 2007. He had attended Master Reiki Tummo and many spiritual retreats in Australia, Singapore and Indonesia under Irmansyah Effendi, founder of Padmachaya, an international organization for spiritual advancement.

Under Padmachaya, Chris has participated in many spiritual fairs in Australia and Singapore,such as Mind, Body, Spirit Festivals, Love and Light Festivals and Holistic Festival. Sharing Divine blessings and spiritual experience with keen fellow meditation practioner and the public is one of Chris’ passion.

He had provided Reiki Healing, guiding others into their spiritual heart and guiding others to their meditative state.
